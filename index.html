Ghost Shooter - The Game

The goal of the game is to get as many points as possible. You have 3 life points. The game ends when you lose all points of life. The Ghost and the Spectre are the main enemies. The enemies move from right to left and from left to right. The Ghost and the Spectre throws bombs. These are the Pumpkin-mask and the Spirit. If one of these bombings hit you, you lose a life. Shoot the Ghost, and you get 50 points. The Spectre is tricky, if you shoot, you lose 60 points. Why? Just. :)

GAME CONTROLS

LEFT ARROW: Move left, RIGHT ARROW: Move right, SPACE: Fire, P KEY: Pause

ENEMIES / BOMBS

Ghost. +50 extra points.

Spectre. -60 points.

Pumpkin & Spirit. +1 point if miss you.
